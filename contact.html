Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

When was Oozma Kappa founded?

Oozma Kappa was founded in 1987.

How many members does Oozma Kappa have?

Officially, Oozma Kappa has six members (University counts members by bodies and not heads, sorry Terri and Terry).

How can I join Oozma Kappa?

It’s easy to join Oozma Kappa! All you have to do is contact us from a method above, stop by a recruitment event, or stop by our house! We’re tons of fun, and we have cake!

Will I have time to be a member of Oozma Kappa?

Only you can decide if you have time to become a brother of Oozma Kappa. We spend most of our time studying, scaring, practicing for the Scare Games, or hanging out with each other.

What types of activities does Oozma Kappa Participate in?

Oozma Kappa participates in various activities on the Monsters University campus, including the Scare Games.

How important are academics in Oozma Kappa?

Academics are a top priority in Oozma Kappa, right after scaring. If members do maintain academic excellence, they may be subject to expulsion from Monsters University by Dean Hardscrabble.

Who is the Oozma Kappa house mom?

Oozma Kappa’s house mom is Sherri Squibbles (Squishy Squibbles mom). According to our brothers, Ms. Squibbles is the best mom in all of Monstropolis.

Who should I contact about membership?

If you have specific questions about Oozma Kappa membership, please contact Mike Wazowski or Don Carlton.
