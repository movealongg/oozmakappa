Join Oozma Kappa

Come home to Oozma Kappa and find friends that turn into brothers for a lifetime. If you look around the Monsters University campus, you will see faces- faces of monsters who are looking for a place to call home. Oozma Kappa brothers are leaders, scholars, and most of all, scarers. The Scaring Program is one of the most brutal collegiate programs in all of Monstropolis. Some monsters are told that “Scariness is the true measure of a monster. If you’re not scary, what kind of monster are you?” The brothers of Oozma Kappa bond on the fact that while they might not be as big or as scary as the members of other fraternities on campus, they are more passionate and more fearless than any monster on the Monsters University campus.

Oozma Kappa is currently made up of six brothers: Mike Wazowski, James P. Sullivan, Terri and Terry Perry, Scott “Squishy” Squibbles, Art, and Don Carlton. Our house mom is Sherri Squibbles, Squishy’s mom. While we are currently petitioning to build a house on Greek Row, our house and our activities are currently held at Sherri Squibbles house off campus.

The brothers of Oozma Kappa are some of the most wholesome, loving, scary monsters that you will ever meet. Navigating through our years at Monsters University is all more bearable because of our bonds of brotherhood. Can you picture yourself here as a brother of Oozma Kappa? We can. What are you waiting for? Join Oozma Kappa today! Let’s show all of Monsters University just how unique and scary every monster can be.
